# Book 1 - Vision

MeatBiz exists to operate the real workflow of wholesale meat businesses:

Supplier purchase -> purchase lot -> meat/parts -> price by customer -> POS bill -> debt -> payment -> report -> next purchase decision.

The long-term target is not only POS. The target is an AI-native industry operating system:

- POS
- inventory
- supplier management
- purchase lots
- customer pricing
- debt and payment
- reporting
- AI assistant
- OCR/voice input
- purchasing recommendation
- inventory forecast
- CEO dashboard

## Product principles

1. Fit real Vietnamese wholesale meat operation first.
2. Use AI only where it reduces human work without reducing control.
3. All money and stock must be traceable.
4. Every historical transaction must remain explainable.
5. Build for mobile/tablet/desktop counter usage.
6. Keep UX fast for night-shift meat business operation.
