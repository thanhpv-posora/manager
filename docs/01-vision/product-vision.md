# MeatBiz Product Vision

MeatBiz is an AI-native operating system for meat wholesalers.

It is not only a POS. It must support the complete business flow:

Supplier → Purchase Lot → Inventory → PriceBook → POS Bill → Debt → Payment → Reports → AI Insight.

## Long-term goal

By V100, MeatBiz should become a vertical AI ERP platform for meat wholesale businesses.
