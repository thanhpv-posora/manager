# MeatBiz Product Vision

## Mission

MeatBiz helps meat wholesalers run daily operations with POS, inventory, supplier, customer debt, pricing, reporting, and AI support.

## Product Category

MeatBiz is not just a POS.

MeatBiz is an AI-native operating system for meat wholesalers.

## Target Users

- Wholesale meat business owners
- Staff who create bills and collect payments
- Suppliers
- Customer/distributor accounts
- Customer sub-accounts / downstream retail customers
- Future AI agents assisting operators

## Strategic Direction

V70: Enterprise hardening and business rule stabilization  
V80: AI operating dashboard and modular monolith  
V90: advanced forecasting, purchasing, and customer portal  
V100: AI-native industry platform
