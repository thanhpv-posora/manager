# MeatBiz V70 Roadmap

## Goal
Move from feature-driven development to governed Enterprise Modular Monolith.

## V70 Priorities
1. P0 security fixes.
2. Scope-based authorization.
3. PriceBook conflict prevention.
4. Payment idempotency table and cancel-create direction.
5. Inventory transaction/snapshot roadmap.
6. Business Bible and ADR adoption.

## Do Not Do Yet
Do not move to microservices in V70. First build a clean modular monolith.
