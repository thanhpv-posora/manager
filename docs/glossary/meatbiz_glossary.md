# MeatBiz Glossary

## Bill
Vietnamese business-facing sales document. In code often represented by `orders`.

## Order
Technical table/entity for bill.

## Payment
Money received from customer.

## Debt
Amount owed by customer after sales and payments.

## PriceBook
Versioned pricing agreement for customer/product/date.

## Customer Tree
Hierarchy of customer and child customers used for scope authorization.

## Unapplied Credit
Overpaid money not yet allocated to any bill.

## Purchase Lot
Supplier purchase batch, often representing livestock or meat lot.

## AI Draft
AI-generated proposed action awaiting human confirmation.
